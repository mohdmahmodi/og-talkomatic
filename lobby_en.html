Create My Own Room?

Each room may have up to 5 participants. We limit the number of chatters to 5 because following 4 other chatters in "real-time" can be quite exhausting!

You may Join a Room that has been created for you. The pre-existing rooms are public and can be joined by up to 4 additional people.

You may also create your own room which has the privacy characteristics you desire. Once you create a room, you may invite others to join you.

Public Rooms are viewable by EVERYONE.

Semi-Private Rooms are opened by a Host who then grants access to the room upon request by others.

Private Rooms require a Key.

When you open a private room, you designate a key that anyone else will have to match EXACTLY in order to enter. Your key can be as simple or as complex as you like, but it is limited to a maximum of 36 characters.
